🌍 Earthquake Fundamentals

🧱 What is an Earthquake?

An earthquake is the sudden release of energy in the Earth's crust, causing seismic waves.

  • Happens due to movement of tectonic plates
  • Energy builds over time → releases suddenly
  • Measured using magnitude (Richter scale)

🌋 How Earthquakes Start

Step-by-step process:

  • Plates move slowly
  • Stress accumulates along faults
  • Rocks break suddenly
  • Energy releases → seismic waves

👉 This is called Elastic Rebound Theory

🌎 Types of Plate Boundaries

Type Description Earthquake Risk
Convergent Plates collide 🔴 High
Divergent Plates separate 🟡 Medium
Transform Plates slide 🔴 High

📍 Why Earthquakes Occur in India

  • Indian Plate collides with Eurasian Plate
  • Himalayan region = HIGH RISK
  • Indo-Gangetic belt = Moderate risk
